Real league schedule — 3 pools of 9

Each pool is a ring: every team plays its two neighbours. Click a link to set who plays first. A team can play only one other at a time, so the first-game links form a matching — and because each pool has an odd number of teams (9), a perfect first round is impossible: someone always waits.
